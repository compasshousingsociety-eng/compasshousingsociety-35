2026 Real Estate Market Forecast

January 15, 2026 5 min read By Sarah Chen 12 Comments

As we step into 2026, the real estate landscape continues to evolve with new opportunities and challenges. After a period of rapid growth and adjustment, the market is showing signs of stabilization with promising trends for both buyers and investors.

Interest Rates & Affordability

Recent signals from the Federal Reserve suggest a more accommodative stance on interest rates. This shift is expected to improve mortgage affordability, potentially bringing more buyers into the market. We're already seeing increased activity in the entry-level and mid-market segments.

Inventory & Supply Dynamics

New construction starts have picked up significantly, particularly in suburban and exurban areas. This influx of new inventory is helping to balance the supply-demand equation, though prime urban locations remain highly competitive.

Emerging Hotspots

Technology hubs and secondary cities are emerging as the new frontiers for real estate investment. Markets like Austin, Nashville, and Raleigh continue to attract both businesses and residents, driving property values upward.

Sustainability & Smart Homes

Energy-efficient and smart home features are no longer just nice-to-haves. They're becoming key differentiators that buyers actively seek. Properties with green certifications are commanding premium prices and selling faster than conventional homes.

What This Means for You

Whether you're a first-time homebuyer, a seasoned investor, or planning to sell, staying informed is crucial. Our team at Compass is here to help you navigate these trends and make confident decisions.
