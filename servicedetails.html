Frequently asked questions

How long does the sales process typically take?

On average, our sales process takes 45 days from listing to closing. This timeline can vary based on market conditions, property type, and buyer demand. We work efficiently to expedite the process while ensuring you get the best value.

What's included in your premium marketing package?

Our premium marketing package includes professional photography, virtual tours, social media campaigns, targeted email marketing, featured listings on major platforms, and premium placement in our network.

Do you offer virtual property tours?

Yes, we provide 3D virtual tours and video walkthroughs for all our listings. This allows potential buyers to explore properties remotely, saving time and increasing engagement.

What areas do you serve?

We serve the greater metropolitan area including Manhattan, Brooklyn, Queens, and surrounding suburbs. Our team has deep local knowledge and extensive networks in these regions.

How do you determine the listing price?

We use a comprehensive approach combining comparative market analysis, recent sales data, property condition assessment, and current market trends. Our data-driven methodology ensures accurate pricing that attracts buyers while maximizing your return.
